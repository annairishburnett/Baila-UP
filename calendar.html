Wednesdays at Dance Zone (1113 Lincoln Ave, Marquette)

Wednesday Lessons and Social dancing are free, donations welcome. No partner required for any of our classes as we rotate partners throughout the class. Social Dancing styles vary by week of the month. See the schedule below in our calendar.

Weekly Social Dancing Styles:
  • 1st week: Salsa
  • 2nd week: East Coast Swing/Lindyhop
  • 3rd week: West Coast Swing
  • 4th week: Ballroom
  • 5th week: Mixed Styles

Tuesdays at NMU (PEIF Dance Studio)

Follows NMU fitness class calendar which closes for college breaks. Click here to see the schedule. Free for PEIF gym members; membership required for entry. Sign up for the class online or check in at the PEIF main desk.
