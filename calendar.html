Baila UP Classes & Events

Wednesday Night Salsa

6:30pm @ Dance Zone, 1113 Lincoln Ave, Marquette

Wednesday Lessons and Social dancing are free, donations welcome. No partner required for any of our classes as we rotate partners throughout the class. Social Dancing styles vary by week of the month. See the schedule below in our calendar. During the summer months the class will be held outdoors, weather permitting. Check our socials for weekly location updates and details! Facebook or Instagram.

Weekly Social Dancing Styles:
  • 1st week: Salsa
  • 2nd week: East Coast Swing/Lindyhop
  • 3rd week: West Coast Swing
  • 4th week: Ballroom
  • 5th week: Mixed Styles

Thursday Night Ballroom Dancing

6:00pm @ NMU PEIF Dance Studio #130

"Smooth Dancing" is taught by Michael Pollock. This class will focus on the basics of ballroom style dancing (Waltz, Tango, Foxtrot, Viennese Waltz, and Argentine Tango) and includes instruction on the frame, movement, and timing of each dance style. Partners are not required, and no experience is necessary.

Thursday Night Salsa Rueda de Casino

8:00pm @ NMU PEIF Dance Studio #130

"Salsa (Rueda)" taught by Melinda Loubser. This is a friendly introduction to social dance that challenges your musicality and your feet! Salsa is a vibrant Latin dance that can be done individually, in pairs, or as in Salsa Rueda de Casino (constantly switching pairs arranged in a circle). In this class you will learn the rhythm and steps of Salsa individually and then move on to Salsa Rueda. Please note, this class will involve some physical contact (hand to hand, hand to back) between participants and partners will be rotated. Partners are not required, and no experience is necessary.
