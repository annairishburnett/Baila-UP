Baila UP Classes & Events

Tuesdays at NMU (PEIF Dance Studio)

8:00pm - Solo Foot Work and Rueda Salsa

This is a friendly introduction to social dance that challenges your musicality and your feet! Salsa is a vibrant Latin dance that can be done individually, in pairs, or as in Salsa Rueda de Casino (constantly switching pairs arranged in a circle). In this class you will learn the rhythm and steps of Salsa individually and then move on to Salsa Rueda. Please note, this class will involve some physical contact (hand to hand, hand to back) between participants and partners will be rotated.

  • Follows NMU fitness class calendar which closes for college breaks. Click here to see the schedule.
  • Free for PEIF gym members; membership required for entry. Sign up for the class online or check in at the PEIF main desk.

Wednesdays at Dance Zone (1113 Lincoln Ave, Marquette)

6:30pm - Beginner Salsa Class
7:00pm - Intermediate Salsa Class
8:15-9:00pm - Social Dancing, styles vary by week

Wednesday Lessons and Social dancing are free, donations welcome. No partner required for any of our classes as we rotate partners throughout the class.

Weekly Social Dancing Styles:
  • 1st week: Salsa
  • 2nd week: East Coast Swing/Lindyhop
  • 3rd week: West Coast Swing
  • 4th week: Ballroom
  • 5th week: Mixed Styles
